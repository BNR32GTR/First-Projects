About Us

TAABO Imports was founded in 2003 by our CEO that just goes by "DAX". He always loved the culture of the Japanese sport cars and decided that he wanted to spread the culture globally. He got his first Japanese car when he was 21 years old. He was very disappointed at how long it took for his car to arrive from Japan. So he made a plan. And as they say "The rest is history".
